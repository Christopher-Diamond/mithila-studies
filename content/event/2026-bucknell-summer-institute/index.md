  This institute brings together an interdisciplinary group of scholars to examine mobile subjects, itinerant genres, and circulating knowledge in the precolonial and colonial worlds of South and Southeast Asia. Participants will workshop pre-circulated papers over three days, with a view to producing a collaborative edited volume or special journal issue.

  The institute is convened by the Mithila Studies Network and hosted by the Bucknell Humanities Center.
